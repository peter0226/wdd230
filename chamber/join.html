Membership Benefits

Select your membership level to enjoy the following benefits:

NP Membership:
  • Access to exclusive events and webinars for non-profit organizations.
  • Receive periodic updates on the latest business regulations for non-profits.
  • Opportunity to collaborate with other non-profit organizations on community initiatives.
Bronze Membership:
  • Access to exclusive networking events for Bronze-level members.
  • Discounts on participation in chamber fairs and expos.
  • Receive quarterly market reports and economic analysis.
Silver Membership:
  • All the benefits of Bronze level.
  • Participation in chamber committees to contribute to decision-making and business policies.
  • Access to personalized business training and development sessions.
Gold Membership:
  • All the benefits of Silver level.
  • Spotlight on the chamber's homepage with a link to your company.
  • Exclusive invitations to dinners and VIP events with local and international business leaders.
  • Opportunity to showcase your company at a chamber event.
